Charm++

parallel programming framework

  • migratable objects

    Express parallelism in terms of interacting collections of objects. Design your classes to represent the units of work and data natural to your app. Dont be shackled by worrying about processors / ranks.

  • asynchronous methods

    Communication is as simple as invoking methods on remote objects. Get zero-effort overlap of your computation with your communication. Define your own serializable data types or message types.

  • adaptive runtime

    Allow our intelligent runtime system to orchestrate execution. You design and decompose the parallel algorithm; the runtime observes and optimizes performance.

capabilities

  • Portable code
  • Automatic overlap
  • Automatic load balancing
  • Automatic checkpointing
  • Resilience to process failures
  • Independent modules, interleaved execution
  • Interoperable with MPI and OpenMP
  • Highly customizable
  • Ecosystem of tools
